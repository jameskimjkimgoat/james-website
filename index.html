Chapter 1

Creative Title: Is an Academic title really necessary to discuss fictional characters in contemporary American literature?

"Nothing else exists for her now beyond this moment, and for her he is nothing more than an arrangement of shapes and colors."

Holden Caulfield, Tyler Durden, Chance the Gardener, Patrick Bateman — four American protagonists, four characters desperately searching for something to grasp in a world that doesn’t make sense.

Their paths diverge, but they’re all looking for a loophole — an escape from society’s fakeness or failure, a way to exist in their own truth.
